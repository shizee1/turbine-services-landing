Welcome to Our Company

TurbineServices.Experts, Inc. was founded and is run by a combination of well experienced and knowledgeable former GE and non-GE personnel with over 70 years of combined experience in the gas turbines field. We have taken great pride in providing our clients the highest quality parts, the technical knowhow and services to ensure that their gas turbines run and operate reliably and efficiently.

Since our incorporation in 2007, we have successfully provided our clients a one stop solution to all of their GE™ industrial gas turbine needs. We have had the pleasure of working with a wide variety of clients all over the world in both the public and private sectors. These customers include electric utilities, service centers, oil & gas companies, original equipment manufacturers such as Thomassen, Alsthom and many more. We are delighted that you have taken the time to visit our web site. We look forward to supporting your current and future spare parts and services' requirements.
